Why MetroHealth?

MetroHealth ranks as one of the best hospitals in the nation for its burn unit and one of the best hospitals in Cleveland overall for patient care. As Cleveland's oldest healthcare provider, it has been providing Clevelanders their healthcare needs since 1837. We have never turned a family away for not being able to pay. This is a Metrohealth promise and commitment to all Clevelanders in every stage of their life.

Employee Wellness

The MetroHealth System is committed to promoting a healthier community, including the well-being of our employees and their families. The award-winning Employee Assistance and Wellness Program team offers programs, resources, and complimentary confidential and professional support so we can care for ourselves to care best for others. Benefit-eligible employees and dependents enrolled on the medical plan can participate in our MetroHealthy Rewards Program to receive a substantial medical plan premium reduction and other incentives throughout the year. The following are just a few options available to our employees and their families:

  • MetroHealthy Wellness Portal
  • Employee Fitness Center at the Main Campus
  • Virtual fitness classes like yoga and bootcamp
  • Employee Assistance Program Counseling
  • Weight management programs
  • Stress management programs
  • Wellness Champion network
  • Special events and health talks
  • Wellness discounts to local gyms
  • Financial wellness programs
  • Free RTA passes through our Healthy Transit Program
  • Clearly labeled healthy menu options in dining locations

Business Resource Groups

Employee Business Resource Groups (EBRGs) are voluntary, employee-led groups that come together around common interests to support each other and provide education and awareness as well as networking, professional development and other opportunities. EBRGs help MetroHealth build relationships with social, cultural and other groups of people who make up our community. They provide opportunities for MetroHealth to share its values of Service to Others, Teamwork, Accountability, Respect, Inclusion and Diversity, and a Quest for Excellence. And they are one of the many ways MetroHealth celebrates diversity, provides a warm, welcoming environment to employees of all backgrounds and fosters their professional and personal success.

Current MetroHealth's EBRGs include:

  • African American Alliance
  • Christian Fellowship
  • Diverse Nurse
  • Emerging Leaders
  • Green Team
  • Hispanic Forum
  • Pride Alliance
  • Veterans Group
  • Wellness Champions
  • Women at MetroHealth
