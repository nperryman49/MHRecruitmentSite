MetroHealth Hospital

Why MetroHealth?

MetroHealth ranks as one of the best hospitals in the nation for its burn unit and one of the best hospitals in Cleveland overall for patient care. As Cleveland's oldest healthcare provider, we literally grew up with the city providing Clevelanders their healthcare needs since 1837. We have never turned a family away for not being able to pay. This is a Metrohealth promise and commitment to all Clevelanders in every stage of their life.

Cleveland, Ohio

Why Cleveland?

At the height of Cleveland's growth it was declared the best location in the nation. That statement holds true to this very day. Cleveland is located in the heart of America and within driving distance to other major cities in all directions. Cleveland is on the rise both figuratively and literally. From the explosive growth of Downtown living to our impecabble local food scene and amazing sports teams, Cleveland has it all for everyone.
